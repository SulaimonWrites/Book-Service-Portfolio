# Book-Service-Portfolio
A professional portfolio showcasing book formatting, editing, and publishing services. This repository demonstrates how I transform manuscripts into polished, market-ready eBooks and print-ready books for authors and publishers.

**About Me**
I am a professional book formatter, editor, and publishing assistant helping authors transform raw manuscripts into polished, market-ready eBooks and print-ready books. My focus is on delivering clean, reader-friendly, and professionally structured books that meet publishing standards.

**Services Offered**
📖 Book Formatting (Kindle eBooks & Paperback)
✍️ Book Editing & Proofreading
🎨 Interior Book Layout Design
📚 eBook Publishing Support (Amazon KDP ready files)
🧾 Manuscript Cleanup & Structuring

**My Workflow**
Receive manuscript from client
Review and structure content
Perform editing and formatting
Design professional book layout (eBook & print-ready)
Quality check for readability & consistency
Deliver final files ready for publishing
